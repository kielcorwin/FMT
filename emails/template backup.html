About Section 2 - Retirement Needs & Expenses

What are your retirement expectations? According to the Employee Benefit Research Institute1, 67% of workers are very or somewhat confident they will have enough money to “live comfortably” throughout retirement. However, only 42% had actually tried to calculate how much they will need to save!

This section contains exercises to help you determine how much money you will need to retire in the future. If you are quickly approaching retirement, we examine whether you can retire today based on the annual income you desire.

1The 2019 Retirement Confidence Survey. Employee Benefit Research Institute.

Who should attend this course?

Retirement Planning Today® contains something for everyone. We address financial issues that pertain to the self-employed as well as employees of corporations and government agencies. The course is designed to teach you how to build wealth and align your money with your values to accomplish your goals in life. Whether you plan to retire 20 years from now or have just recently retired, the information you learn in this class can deliver rewards throughout your lifetime.
